"Do what you like, not what you love."

This saying was fairly apt for my situation. As I took on more commissions I realized it
was bleeding away my passion for my hobby. At the same time, I ended up developing
carpal tunnel in both of my wrists and would experience a lot of back pain due to my shrimp-like
posture while sitting at the computer.

I went on a hiatus, save for two art classes in community college. I realized I enjoyed programming
there after taking an introductory computer science class, and that lead to me majoring in computer
science here at Western. I would maybe only finish one illustration per year for about two years.
