COCA-COLA DREAMWORLD

VERDICT: 7.5/10

This is one of Coca-Cola's limited edition sodas, and came out in 2022. I was recommended that I try this by a friend of mine, despite my disinterest, and I ended up enjoying it quite a bit.

It is definitely very sweet, and the main flavors seen to be a fruity, mango/raspberry-like flavor along with the typical notes of Coca-Cola, which is somewhat disjointed but surprisingly pleasant.
