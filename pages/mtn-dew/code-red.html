MTN DEW CODE RED

VERDICT: 8/10

Unlike it's grape flavored cousin, Code Red actually lives up to its name and is vibrantly, concerningly red. It's concentrated and vibrant, and has a bit of tartness owing to the cherry flavor and its base as a MTN Dew beverage. I do not reach for it often, but it is pretty good and one of the better cherry sodas I have tried.
