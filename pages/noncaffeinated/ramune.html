RAMUNE

VERDICT: 8.5/10

There are a lot of different flavors of Ramune, but Strawberry is generally one of my favorites. While it isn't necessarily true to what strawberry actually tastes like, it is sweet and vibrant and the little marble in the top of the bottle is fun to roll around.
