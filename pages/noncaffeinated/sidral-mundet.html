HOME COLAS CAFFEINATED NON-CAFFEINATED MTN DEW SITE MAP

SIDRAL MUNDET

VERDICT: 8/10

I usually don't see a lot of appreciation for apple-flavored sodas, and its seemingly one of the last flavors that a company will considering when deciding what flavor they're going to add to their lineup next. However, this one is pretty good.

I first encountered it in a Mexican restaurant, lined up next to the glass bottles of Coca-Cola. The flavor is sweet and fairly mellow.
