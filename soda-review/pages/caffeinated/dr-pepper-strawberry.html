DR PEPPER STRAWBERRIES & CREAM

VERDICT: 5.5/10

"Too much of a good thing is a bad thing" is a very applicable saying to this beverage. Each sip was such an intense blast of flavor that it took me several hours to finish a personal bottle.

There is so much going on in this drink that it is difficult to make sense of it on your tongue, and when you do finally get used it to it, it just feels like a Dr Pepper poured out of a Freestyle machine that had not been cleaned in several months.
