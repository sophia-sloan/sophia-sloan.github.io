COCA COLA STARLIGHT

VERDICT: -2/10

This beverage tastes like a lot of things. None of them are good. It tastes like it came out of a Wendy's freestyle machine, only to be condemned to sitting in a hot car for three days, repackaged after dissolving a heap of cotton candy into it, and sold as the first of Coca-Cola's limited edition "Coca-Cola Creations" in 2022 AD.

This is the kind of beverage you give to someone if you want to mess with them (this is what happened to me, by the way). Something needs to be done about whoever greenlit this.
