MTN DEW BAJA BLAST

VERDICT: 10/10

I am biased. I really enjoy Baja Blast. You could fill an olympic swimming pool with how much Baja Blast I have consumed. I would stockpile Baja Blast cans during the brief period they would be available in grocery stores.

It tastes like fruity, tropical sand. It's really good. Usually I prefer eating my food with water or some other drink with less of a distinct profile but it pairs alright with Taco Bell's "let's just pretend that it's Mexican" menu.

The only caveat is that if you are purchasing it at a Taco Bell, there is a 50% chance that they will water it down, in which case it tastes pretty disgusting. It is at its strongest in a can or a bottle.
