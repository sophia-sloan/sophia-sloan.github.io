FANTA MELON

VERDICT: 10/10

One of the best soft drinks of all time, but I have never seen this being sold outside of Japan, and with how much customs cost you might as well just book a flight and check a bag for smuggling a few cases home with you.

This beverage is a soothing, cool honeydew flavor, which is one of my favorites if you happen to be stuck outside in over ninety degree weather.
