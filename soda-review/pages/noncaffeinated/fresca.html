HOME COLAS CAFFEINATED NON-CAFFEINATED MTN DEW SITE MAP

FRESCA

VERDICT: 8/10

This is a really refreshing beverage, but it's grapefruit flavored, so it's also pretty sour. One of the main marketing appeals of this soda was that its a "diet soda", but in true American fashion that proved to not be very appealing to consumers, so there's plenty of sweeter variants.

It tastes more like a romanticized and candied version of grapefruit, which is fine by me since I normally do not care for how grapefruit tastes to begin with.
