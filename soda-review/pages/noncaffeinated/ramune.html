RAMUNE

VERDICT: 9.5/10

This brand is more known for their sparkling water, but these are as refreshing as they are pricy. The little hat the cans come with definitely sell the feeling of decadence.

The limoncello flavor packs a pretty serious punch and tastes fairly similar to its namesake, besides not being alcoholic. The blood orange has a rich, juicy flavor, and a faint thickness from the pulp of the oranges.
